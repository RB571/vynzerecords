VYNZΞ RECORDS is an independent electronic music label founded by Aron Vynz, focusing on futuristic soundscapes and immersive storytelling. Bridging melodic techno, progressive house, and ambient electronica, the label connects music, visuals, and narrative into one visionary experience. Its releases — from Pulse to Schoolwork — reflect a mission to amplify vision through sound.

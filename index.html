Aron Vynz

Aron Vynz is an electronic producer and DJ blending melodic techno and progressive house with futuristic soundscapes. His music is defined by deep basslines, shimmering melodies, and an immersive, cinematic atmosphere. With Pulse, Aron Vynz presents his vision: music as energy, as a signal, as a calling.

Redemption

Redemption is a collaboration project between Northsnd and Megapete. Founded in the year 2000, Redemption became known for its signature fusion of classic trance and hard-edged dance sounds. After a 25-year journey, the project concludes with Legacy — a definitive collection featuring their greatest tracks.

Shogun DJ Squad

Shogun DJ Squad is a collaboration project founded in 1996 and rooted in the golden era of happy hardcore. Blending euphoric melodies, breakbeats, and pure rave energy, the project embodies the spirit of late-90s dance culture. In 2025, they returned with Hakkuh Helden, a new EP celebrating the enduring power of the hardcore sound.

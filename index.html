Artists

VYNZΞ

VYNZΞ is the electronic project of producer and DJ Aron Vynz. Blending melodic techno, progressive house, and futuristic sound design, VYNZΞ crafts immersive worlds built on deep basslines, shimmering melodies, and a cinematic sense of scale. With PULSE, Aron Vynz unveils his core artistic concept: music as energy — as a signal.

Northsnd

Northsnd has been a pioneer in electronic dance music since 1996. Schoolwork revisits his formative years (1996–2014) across five volumes and 100 tracks — spanning happy hardcore, dreamhouse, trance, and techno — reimagined with modern production while preserving the raw spirit of the originals.

Redemption

Redemption is a collaboration project between Northsnd and Megapete. Founded in the year 2000, Redemption became known for its signature fusion of classic trance and hard-edged dance sounds. After a 25-year journey, the project concludes with Legacy — a definitive collection featuring their greatest tracks.

AVYLA

AVYLA is the new melodic-trance identity within the VYNZΞ universe. Founded by electronic producer Aron Vynz, AVYLA represents a personal vision: trance not only as a genre, but as a state of mind — uplifting, atmospheric, and deeply immersive.  AVYLA’s music aims to transport listeners into bright, expansive soundscapes where emotion leads the way.
